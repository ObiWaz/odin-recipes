Leveled Up Instant Ramen

It's food!

Instant ramen doesn't have to be a shameful experience. I mean, yeah we're poor, and capitilism forces us to eat copius amounts of carbs and salt to satiate our dopamine needs. However, we can accelerate that dopamine flow with a few tweaks!

Ingredients

Below are a list of things to add to your ramen and when in the cooking process to add them.

  • Fried Egg - right at the end on top of the bowl of Ramen.
  • Poached egg - crack an egg a couple minutes before the ramen is done.
  • Sesame oil - right at the end before transfering to the bowl....Unless your bowl is the pot. That is okay too. There is no judgement here.
  • Green onions - right at the end.
  • Slice of American cheese (yes it is good) - right at the end.
  • Splash of wine, rice vinegar, or sake - right at the end.
  • Sriracha - I like to put it right at the beginning, but any time is fine.
  • Bean sprouts - 30 seconds before done.
  • Teaspon of curry powder - Right at the beginning. This is awesome by the way.
  • Tofu - Right at the beginning.
