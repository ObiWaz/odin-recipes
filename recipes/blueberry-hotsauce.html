Blueberry Hotsauce

Great on everything.

This is a very easy hotsauce to make. The recipe calls for Trinidad Scorpions, but in a pinch habaneros work just as well.

Ingredients

  • 6-10 scorpion peppers. Or as many as you think you need.
  • 1 lb of blueberries
  • 2 green onions
  • 1/3 cup of brown sugar
  • Juice of 1 orange
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 2/3 cup of vinegar.

Making the sauce

  1. Chop the peppers and scallions
  2. Put everything into a sauce pan except the lime and orange juice.
  3. Let simmer at low/medium or medium heat for 15 minutes.
  4. Add juice of lime/orange at the end.
  5. Let it cool.
  6. Hit the entire mixture with a stick blender, or put it all into a food processor.
  7. Store in refrigerator for up to 10 days.
